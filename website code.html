About the Project üìñ

Poor posture, especially during extended periods of sitting, can contribute to several health issues including back pain, neck strain, and muscle stiffness. A smart posture corrector combines a traditional brace with advanced sensor technology. This innovative design detects improper posture in the neck and back, monitors the duration and frequency of maintaining proper posture, and identifies instances of leaning forward.

Our posture detector will detect those different types of wrong sitting postures using couple of sensors and electronic components. The treatment requires from the patient wearing this smart posture corrector for 20 minutes per day.

Hardware Design üõ†Ô∏è

The components we used in our project are: IMU,x3 Flex Sensors (3 long & 1 short), Two Types of Press Buttons, x1 LED, x3 10K resistors, x1 LCD, x1 Buzzer, x1 Vibration Motor, x1 Battery, x1 DC to DC converter. Note that the height and weight affects our work where the brace's size changes accordingly.

Software Design üíª

We used Labview for calibration, in addition to Arduino IDE to write the code and test the functionalities of our system.

The code of each component was tested alone, then we combined the different code files and added the thresholds that after reaching them the Vibration Motor/LED will turn on showing a wrong posture. Plug and play calibration was implemented to ease the use of this posture corrector and fit different people who might use it.
